There's a version of this garden that tries to be complete. Every command
fully implemented, every edge case handled, every future feature carefully
scoped. That version never gets planted, because planting requires
letting go of the imagined version.

The garden I'm actually building is the half-finished one. It has a graph
that doesn't always settle. A letter that's sometimes a little too
tender. A cross command that occasionally finds links that aren't
really there. These are the things that make it feel like a garden and
not a product.

The half-finished thing is the living thing.
